1) Effiel Tower

  • Cost: Free
  • Target Age: Anyone

A lift to the peak of the Eiffel Tower can squeeze the budget, but views from below can be equally stunning.
With beautiful lawns and flowerbeds, this area is best for enjoying a pinic either solo or with family and friends!


2) Museum of Modern Art

  • Cost: Free
  • Target Age: Anyone

For a surreal view of French culture, dive into the permanent collections at the Museum of Modern Art. One of Paris's many free museums!


3) Pont d'Arcole

  • Cost: Free
  • Target Age: Adults

Want to take a lovely night stroll after watching Somethings Gotta Give? Check out this beautiful bridge where actor Jack Nicoleson, lamented over his life's choices saying: "Look who gets to be the girl!"
